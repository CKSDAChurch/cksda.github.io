Young Adults

A community of young adults (25–35) committed to fellowship, Bible study, and service.

Our Young Adults ministry brings together post-collegiate young professionals for meaningful connection rooted in faith. We gather for prayer meetings, social events, service projects, and Bible study throughout the year.

Whether you are new to Collegedale or have been attending for years, you are welcome here. Reach out to learn more about upcoming events and how to get plugged in.

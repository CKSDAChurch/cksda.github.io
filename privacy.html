Privacy Policy

Effective date: May 29, 2026

Who We Are

This website is operated by Collegedale Korean Seventh-day Adventist Church ("CKSDA Church", "we", "us"), located in Collegedale, TN. Questions about this policy can be directed to contact-us@cksda.church.

What We Collect and Why

We use two third-party analytics tools to understand how visitors use our site. We do not sell your data. No analytics are activated until you explicitly accept cookies via the consent banner.

Google Analytics 4 (GA4)

Provided by Google LLC. When you accept cookies, GA4 collects:

  • Pages visited and time spent on each page
  • General geographic region (country/city level — not precise location)
  • Device type, browser, and operating system
  • How you arrived at the site (e.g., search engine, direct link)

Data is processed by Google and subject to Google's Privacy Policy. Google retains raw event data for up to 14 months by default. You can opt out of Google Analytics tracking at any time using the Google Analytics Opt-out Browser Add-on.

Microsoft Clarity

Provided by Microsoft Corporation. When you accept cookies, Clarity collects:

  • Anonymized session replays (mouse movements, clicks, scroll depth)
  • Aggregated heatmap data
  • Pages visited and time on page

Clarity is configured to mask all text input fields and does not capture passwords or personal data you type. Data is processed by Microsoft and subject to Microsoft's Privacy Statement. Microsoft retains Clarity data for 13 months. You can opt out via Microsoft's privacy dashboard.

Cookies

We use the following cookies on this site:

  • cksdaConsent — Stores your cookie consent choice (accepted or declined). Set by this website. Persists in localStorage until cleared.
  • _ga, _ga_* — Google Analytics cookies used to distinguish users and sessions. Set only when you accept. Expires after 2 years.
  • _clsk, _clckid — Microsoft Clarity cookies used to identify sessions. Set only when you accept. Expires after 1 day and 1 year respectively.

You can manage or delete cookies at any time through your browser settings. Clearing your browser's localStorage will reset your consent choice, and the banner will reappear on your next visit.

Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have rights under applicable privacy law (including GDPR, CCPA, and UK GDPR) to:

  • Access, correct, or request deletion of data collected about you
  • Withdraw consent at any time (clear your browser localStorage to reset your choice, and decline when the banner reappears)
  • Object to or restrict processing
  • Lodge a complaint with your local data protection authority

Because analytics data is aggregated and pseudonymous and held by Google/Microsoft, most requests to access or delete individual analytics data should be directed to those providers using the opt-out links above.

Data Security

This site is hosted on GitHub Pages over HTTPS. We do not store personal data on our own servers. All analytics data is stored and secured by Google and Microsoft under their respective data protection commitments.

Children

This site is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children.

Children's Ministries

A nurturing, faith-filled environment for kids of all ages, from newborns to high schoolers.

Our Children's Ministries team provides age-appropriate Sabbath School classes and special programs designed to help kids grow in their relationship with God. Classes meet every Sabbath morning during the Sabbath School hour (10:00–11:00 AM) and are led by caring, dedicated volunteers.

We serve kids of all ages — from newborns to high schoolers. Once students graduate, they are welcomed into our Collegiate Ministry. We welcome children of all backgrounds and love seeing families grow together in faith. If you would like to volunteer or learn more, please reach out.
